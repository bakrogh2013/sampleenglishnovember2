My Morning in Ankara

Level A1–A2 • Reading time 2–3 minutes • Keyboard friendly

Every morning, my family and I eat breakfast together. After breakfast, I walk to school with my brother. Our house is near the market, and the streets are busy. At school, I learn math and English. After class, I go home and help my mother cook dinner. In the evening, we watch a show and talk about our day. We live in Ankara, and I like our neighborhood.
